The "Youth Cup" by MQ Technology is an innovative non-electrolytic hydrogen-rich cup designed for modern individuals pursuing a healthy lifestyle. Without the need for electricity or an external power source, this product employs advanced non-electrolytic technology to rapidly produce high-concentration hydrogen-rich water through the natural interaction of materials and water, offering an antioxidant health experience anytime, anywhere.

Key Features

  • Non-Electrolytic Technology: Uses special mineral materials to naturally release hydrogen — no batteries or charging needed
  • High Hydrogen Concentration: 800-1200ppb in a short time, effectively neutralizing free radicals
  • Portable Design: Lightweight, 300-400ml capacity — perfect for office, gym, or travel
  • Durable Materials: Food-grade 304 stainless steel and high-quality Tritan
  • Youthful Vitality: Hydrogen-rich water boosts metabolism and rejuvenates the body
