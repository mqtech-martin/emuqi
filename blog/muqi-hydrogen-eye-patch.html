Solid-state hydrogen technology delivering deep antioxidant repair to delicate eye skin. 92.3% dermal penetration, clinically proven results in 4 weeks.

MuQi's solid-state hydrogen eye patch combines HI Hydrogen Embedding Technology with a medical-grade carrier matrix. Clinical data shows 18.7% elasticity improvement and 23.4% wrinkle reduction in 4 weeks — a shift from surface hydration to deep antioxidant repair.
