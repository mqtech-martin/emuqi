Community Hydrogen-Rich Water Station by Muqi Technology

June 3, 2025 · Martin Chen · Products

A new generation of community hydrogen-rich water stations — 3D hydrogen production technology meeting daily healthy drinking needs.

Muqi Technology's community hydrogen-rich water stations combine 3D hydrogen production technology with smart ecosystem management. PEM electrolysis + nano-bubble dissolution + mineral ceramic hydrogen-locking core deliver truly effective healthy drinking water.

Driven by the awakening of health consciousness and concerns over water quality safety, traditional water purifiers can no longer meet consumers' advanced needs for healthy drinking water. Muqi Technology, with its innovative 3D hydrogen production technology, has introduced a new generation of community hydrogen-rich water stations.

Core Technology: 3D Hydrogen Production

PEM Electrolysis + Nano-Bubble Dissolution

Utilizing PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) water electrolysis technology, the system provides a pure hydrogen source, ensuring high and stable hydrogen concentration. Combined with high-pressure turbine technology, efficient nano-bubble hydrogen dissolution allows hydrogen to fully dissolve in water and resist escape.

Patented Mineral Ceramic Hydrogen-Lock Core

The patented mineral ceramic membrane hydrogen-rich core acts like a "hydrogen molecule safe," locking in high concentrations of hydrogen and optimizing water quality. The hydrogen-rich water features five characteristics: weak alkalinity, small molecule cluster structure, rich in trace minerals, high dissolved oxygen, and excellent taste.

FAQ

How does the hydrogen-locking core work?

The patented mineral ceramic membrane uses micro-porous structure to stabilize dissolved hydrogen, preventing escape and maintaining concentration for extended periods after dispensing.

What is the hydrogen concentration?

The system maintains consistent hydrogen concentration above 800ppb, well above the therapeutic threshold of 300ppb for antioxidant benefits.

Is the station suitable for commercial deployment?

Yes. The station is designed for community and commercial use with built-in payment processing, usage analytics, and remote management capabilities.
