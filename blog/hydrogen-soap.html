Second-Gen Hydrogen Soap for Sensitive Skin

February 10, 2025 · Martin Chen · Products

Advanced solid hydrogen technology with 1000ppb hydrogen content — repairing sensitive skin through hydrogen-rich foam.

MQ Second-Gen Hydrogen Soap — featuring advanced solid hydrogen technology with 1000ppb hydrogen content. Hydrogen-rich foam provides proven sensitive skin repair. Weak alkaline pH, dense foam, jasmine fragrance.

Since the successful development and launch of hydrogen soap by the MQ Solid Hydrogen Laboratory in 2022, the product has gained increasing recognition from customers worldwide. With its unique experience and innovative category, the hydrogen soap has become a consumer product with an extremely high repurchase rate.

Key Features

Hydrogen-Rich Foam

The soap produces dense, abundant foam with dissolved molecular hydrogen. When applied to skin, the hydrogen molecules penetrate the epidermis to neutralize free radicals at the cellular level, providing genuine skin repair rather than just surface cleansing.

Product Variants

The hydrogen soap line includes multiple formulations to address different skin concerns — from the signature jasmine-scented formula for daily use to specialized variants for acne-prone skin, body wash, and facial cleansing. Each variant maintains the core hydrogen technology while optimizing for its specific application.

FAQ

How does hydrogen help sensitive skin?

Molecular hydrogen selectively neutralizes harmful free radicals that cause inflammation and skin damage, while preserving beneficial signaling molecules. This reduces redness, irritation, and long-term sensitivity.

How long does the hydrogen effect last in the foam?

Hydrogen remains active in the foam for several minutes after lathering, which is sufficient for the cleansing and treatment process.

Is the soap suitable for facial use?

Yes. The weak alkaline pH and dense foam make it gentle enough for facial cleansing while providing the benefits of hydrogen therapy.
