The Next Big Opportunity for Patch Factories:
Why Hydrogen Patches?

Jul 13, 2026 · Martin Chen · Industry Insight

281 billion yuan market, homogenized competition, and a category gap — solid-state hydrogen patches check three boxes for manufacturers.

写在前面（结论先行）：外用贴膏剂行业正在同质化竞争的深渊中内卷——降价、换包装、再投流，三个动作循环了三年。但有一个方向，兼具前沿物理特性验证、品类认知空白和供应链可落地三个条件——氢敷贴。

Before we begin — the conclusion first: The topical patch industry has been trapped in a cycle of price cuts, repackaging, and ad spending for three years. But one direction checks three boxes — validated physical properties, zero category awareness, and ready-to-use supply chain: embedding solid-state hydrogen material into patches to create the hydrogen patch.

— MQ Hydrogen Foot Patch product series — from conventional patches to hydrogen patches, sharing the same production line

01
PART

A ¥28.1B Market — Why the Struggle?

一、281 亿的市场，为什么越做越难？

In 2025, China's topical patch market reached approximately ¥28.1 billion (industry estimates), with a compound annual growth rate exceeding 10%. The market is still expanding, but each of the three sub-sectors faces its own bottleneck:

SegmentShareGrowth StatusCore Challenge
TCM Patches~65%Stable existing volumeLong reformulation cycles, difficult transdermal efficiency gains, allergy concerns
Pharma Gel Patches~35%Profitable but accelerating homogenizationStar product imitation cycle shortened to 12–18 months, prices driven below 70%
General Health PatchesNo clear stats~12% annual growthLow tech barriers, low ASP, consumers see no reason to pay more

— Structural overview of the three sub-sectors in the topical patch market

This isn't a single factory's problem — it's the collective anxiety of the entire topical patch industry.

02
PART

Why "Hydrogen + Patch" Deserves a Hard Look

二、为什么"氢+敷贴"值得认真看

Three dimensions converge into a structural opportunity.

Hydrogen's Transdermal Potential: A Gradually Validated Physical Property
氢分子的透皮潜力：一个被持续验证的物理特性

Hydrogen is the smallest molecule in nature — this physical property gives it天然想象空间 in transdermal delivery. In recent years, international academic journals have featured frequent research on transdermal hydrogen delivery, from microneedle patches to pressure-assisted delivery. This is not a "future concept" in papers — it's a technology direction being progressively validated by the academic community.

— Solid-state hydrogen carrier microscopic structure — hydrogen gas stably locked in nano-porous mineral carriers

A Near-Empty Category Window
品类认知几乎空白，窗口期已开

At the market level, keyword awareness for "hydrogen patch" is nearly zero. Category空白期 won't last long — once 2–3 brands start building channels, content, and mindshare, the cost of entry for latecomers will multiply.

KEY POINT

Category空白 ≠ conceptual innovation — it's the supply side's delayed response. The hydrogen patch opportunity isn't about "educating consumers on a new concept" — it's about using already-industrialized solid-state hydrogen technology to add a substantive upgrade variable to a mature category.

03
PART

From One-Way to Two-Way: A Category Logic Extension

三、从"单一交互"到"双向交互"：一次品类逻辑的延伸

Traditional foot patches have operated around the "moisture absorption & detox" positioning for three decades. This positioning sustained the market but also created structural limitations — user expectation is limited to "absorption," and repurchase motivation beyond habit lacks a sufficiently strong reason.

Introducing "hydrogen" into patches doesn't negate the old category — it creates a category logic extension:

DimensionTraditional Patch LogicHydrogen Patch Iteration
User ExperiencePassive one-way (absorption/detox)Active interaction (continuous hydrogen release + traditional sensation)
Tech BarrierBamboo vinegar + mineral powder adsorptionSolid-state hydrogen carrier + controlled-release process, significantly higher barrier
Repurchase ReasonHabit-driven, weak stickinessNew sensory anchor and expression space
Channel LeveragePrice competition, limited brand premiumNew category positioning, window for repricing

— Hydrogen patch product concept and positioning

The essence of this leap: not competing with "treatment," but redefining the market with "technology upgrade + category innovation."

Traditional patches accumulated 30 years of category awareness — this doesn't need to be torn down. The hydrogen patch direction adds a value increment on top of existing awareness, letting an "old track" grow a "new species."

04
PART

Tech Is Ready: Solid-State Hydrogen, From Lab to Line

四、技术已就位：固态氢从论文走进产线

Whether a new technology can be rapidly deployed depends on three things: material stability, process reproducibility, and supply chain maturity.

Over the past two years, MQ's solid-state hydrogen lab focused on core R&D, achieving ICR Intelligent Controlled Release technology — locking hydrogen gas stably in nano-porous mineral ceramic carriers, enabling time-sequenced gradient release under specific conditions.

STEP 01

ICR Technology Industrialization Path

01 Solid-state hydrogen powder preparation → Nano-porous carrier搭载 hydrogen molecules, stable lock-in 02 Powder grading & formula adaptation → Adjust release curves per patch substrate, compatible with existing formulas 03 Direct production line mixing → No line changes, no new equipment — simply add at the mixing stage in proportion 04 Finished product testing & QC → Quantitative hydrogen content detection, batch consistency guaranteed

— Hydrogen foot patch product detail shots

Most patch factories care not about theory but "can my existing line use it" — the answer is yes.

NOTE

The keyword is "interlayer implantation" — solid-state hydrogen material is embedded as an independent layer, without interfering with the original backing, adhesive, or release system. This is exactly why it's compatible with existing production lines.

A Practical Action Plan for Patch Makers

五、敷贴工厂的行动建议

If you run a patch/adhesive/foot patch production line, consider "hydrogen patch" as a low-risk upgrade option:

1

Verify first, scale later: Use existing samples for small-batch "hydrogenation" trials; test hydrogen concentration release curves;

2

Let data speak: Pair with third-party test reports to turn differentiation into a verifiable selling point;

3

Find the right tech partner: Solid-state hydrogen material form, implantation process, and release curves are the technical core of this step.

TIMELINE

Not a new production line — just one extra "hydrogenation" step on your existing line.

A ¥28.1 billion market doesn't lack followers. What it lacks is someone brave enough to define the new category first.
