H2fizz Cup: Redefining Healthy Drinking with Hydrogen Technology

The world's first portable disposable hydrogen-rich water cup — one tear, one soak, 3 seconds to antioxidant-rich water.

H2fizz Cup — the world's first portable disposable hydrogen-rich water cup developed by MuQi Technology. One tear, one soak, 3 seconds to 1500ppb hydrogen-rich water. No power supply, no filter element, no waiting.

"H2fizz Cup" cleverly combines hydrogen (H2) and "fizz" (the sound of bubbles), conveying the product's characteristics of hydrogen production and bubble generation. Health Benefits

Molecular hydrogen (H₂) is a selective antioxidant that neutralizes harmful free radicals while preserving beneficial signaling molecules. Regular consumption of hydrogen-rich water has been associated with:

  • Reduced oxidative stress and inflammation
  • Improved athletic recovery and reduced muscle fatigue
  • Enhanced skin health and anti-aging effects
  • Better sleep quality and cognitive function

FAQ

How long does the hydrogen effect last?

Hydrogen concentration remains above 800ppb for approximately 30-60 minutes after preparation. For maximum benefit, drink within 15 minutes.

Is the cup safe for children?

Yes. The hydrogen material is food-grade and non-toxic. The cup is safe for all ages, including children and pregnant women.

Can I use it with cold water?

Hot water (50-65°C) activates hydrogen fastest, but the cup also works with room temperature and cold water, just with slightly longer activation time.
