MQ Tech: Solid Hydrogen Reshapes Industrial Ecosystem

February 15, 2025 · Martin Chen · Industry

MQ Technology's core strategy: solid hydrogen materials and product supply chain across health, agriculture, beauty, and medical applications.

MQ Technology, a domestic leading enterprise in solid hydrogen technology, is focused on the "solid hydrogen materials and product supply chain" — promoting hydrogen energy applications across health, agriculture, beauty, and medical care.

Recently, MQ Technology announced its core strategy centered on solid hydrogen materials and product supply chain development. By breaking through technology barriers and laying out the industrial ecosystem, the company is promoting hydrogen energy applications across multiple scenarios — responding to national hydrogen energy industry policy while opening new growth space under health consumption upgrade trends.

Policy and Market Drivers

With the implementation of the "Medium- and Long-term Development Plan for Hydrogen Energy Industry (2021-2035)," solid hydrogen technology has become a key breakthrough for commercial hydrogen application. Its advantages — high safety, low storage and transportation costs — make it ideal for consumer products.

Health Sector

MQ's hydrogen health products include hydrogen water generators, hydrogen tablets, hydrogen eye patches, hydrogen face masks, and hydrogen soap — forming a complete consumer health ecosystem. Each product leverages solid-state hydrogen technology for sustained, controlled H₂ release.

Agriculture Sector

Hydrogen-enriched water for irrigation has shown improved crop yield, enhanced root development, and increased stress resistance. MQ's ceramic hydrogen tablets for agricultural use provide an affordable, scalable solution for farmers.

Beauty Sector

The beauty division offers hydrogen-infused skincare products including eye patches, face masks, and soap. These products combine MQ's proprietary HI Hydrogen Embedding Technology with cosmetic-grade ingredients for clinically validated anti-aging results.

Medical Sector

MQ's medical applications include hydrogen inhalation devices, hydrogen-rich saline, and solid-state hydrogen wound dressings. These products are designed for clinical settings, supporting post-surgical recovery and chronic disease management.

Supply Chain Integration
