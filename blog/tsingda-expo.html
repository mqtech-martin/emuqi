24th TsingDa Health Industry Expo by MQ Technology

February 7, 2025 · Martin Chen · Events

MQ Technology showcases hydrogen medicine, health, and energy products at the 24th TsingDa Health Industry Expo in Beijing.

MQ Technology cordially invites you to the 24th TsingDa Health Industry Expo at the Beijing National Agricultural Exhibition Hall. Visit us at Hall 11, Booth B48 to experience our latest hydrogen health innovations.

We sincerely invite you to participate in the upcoming 24th TsingDa Health Industry Expo. At this grand event, MQ Technology will showcase our latest achievements in hydrogen medicine, hydrogen health, hydrogen energy consumer products, and hydrogen health care products and technologies.

Exhibition Details

  • Event: 24th TsingDa Health Industry Expo
