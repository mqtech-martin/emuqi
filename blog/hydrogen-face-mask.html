Hydrogen Face Mask: Skin Rejuvenation from Muqi Technology

February 8, 2025 · Martin Chen · Products

HI Hydrogen Embedding Technology delivers 30+ minutes of sustained hydrogen release for deep skin rejuvenation.

MuQi Hydrogen Face Mask — powered by HI Hydrogen Embedding Technology. Solid-state hydrogen embedded in mask fibers releases continuously for 30+ minutes, delivering deep skin rejuvenation through sustained antioxidant delivery.

In the modern skincare field, technological innovation is constantly driving the evolution of beauty products. The Hydrogen Face Mask by MuQi Technology integrates advanced solid-state hydrogen technology — representing a new concept in skincare and a revolution in the beauty industry.

Advanced Technology

HI Hydrogen Embedding

The mask uses HI hydrogen embedding technology, which embeds solid-state hydrogen (HI) into the fibers or layers of the facial mask through ion plating technology. This allows the mask to continuously release hydrogen when in contact with water, bringing a sustained skincare experience.

Controlled Release System

Hydrogen-controlled release technology ensures the speed and amount of hydrogen release can be precisely controlled, with release time exceeding 30 minutes. This extended duration allows the skin to fully absorb hydrogen for lasting skincare effects.

How to Use

Cleanse face and apply the mask. The solid-state hydrogen activates upon contact with skin moisture, releasing hydrogen continuously for over 30 minutes. Remove mask and gently pat remaining essence into skin. Use 2-3 times per week for optimal results.

FAQ

How is this different from regular face masks?

Regular masks deliver static ingredients. MuQi's hydrogen mask dynamically releases molecular hydrogen over 30+ minutes, providing sustained antioxidant treatment that penetrates deeper than surface-level hydration.

Can I use it after cosmetic procedures?

Yes. The mask is designed for post-treatment recovery. The gentle hydrogen release soothes inflammation and accelerates healing without irritating compromised skin.

How often should I use the mask?

For general skincare, 2-3 times per week. For post-procedure recovery, daily use is recommended for the first week, then reduce to 2-3 times weekly.
