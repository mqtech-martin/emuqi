Solutions

Custom hydrogen and ceramic material solutions for agriculture and industry.

Hydrogen agriculture is an emerging field that leverages hydrogen-rich water to improve crop yield, enhance plant resistance, and reduce the need for chemical fertilizers and pesticides.

Muqi Tech provides hydrogen-generating ceramic materials specifically designed for agricultural applications:

  • Crop Growth Enhancement: Hydrogen-rich water irrigation promotes root development and nutrient absorption
  • Stress Resistance: Hydrogen water helps plants withstand drought, salinity, and temperature stress
  • Preservation: Hydrogen-rich water extends shelf life of harvested fruits and vegetables
  • Soil Improvement: Reduces oxidative stress in soil, promoting beneficial microbial activity
  • Reduced Chemical Input: Hydrogen water can reduce the need for pesticides and chemical fertilizers
