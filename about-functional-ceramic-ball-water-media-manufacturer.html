Material science · Application supply chain

Building the solid-state hydrogen application ecosystem.

Muqi Technology is a hydrogen energy technology company focused on the research, development, and application of new solid-state hydrogen storage and generation materials. We are committed to advancing innovative uses of solid-state hydrogen in health, medical care, and agriculture.

Through our “Hydrogen+” strategy, we empower traditional industries including daily chemical products, non-woven materials, Chinese herbal medicine, and water purification. Muqi is a leading company in consumer-facing solid-state hydrogen applications.

From new materials to finished products, Muqi connects scientific research, manufacturing, intellectual property, and application development in one solid-state hydrogen platform.

02 / Research foundation

A complete research and innovation path.

Muqi has established a laboratory for new solid-state hydrogen storage and generation materials. The R&D team includes 12 researchers, with master’s degree holders or above accounting for 60% of the team.

Over the years, Muqi has built a complete product innovation model through industry–university–research collaboration:

2017 — Developed the Hydrogen Water Ceramic product series through the Key Laboratory of Eco-Environmental Functional Materials of the Ministry of Education.

2019 — Developed solid-state hydrogen materials and application products in collaboration with the Institute of Physical and Chemical Technology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

2021 — Established the Hydrogen Product Innovation Center with Shandong University of Technology.

03 / Manufacturing discipline

Turning research into a complete product supply chain.

Muqi has developed a complete supply chain for solid-state hydrogen products. As a leading company in the category, Muqi developed the solid-state hydrogen raw material HI Hydrogen and used it to create Hydrogen Water Ceramic, hydrogen water cups, hydrogen Chinese herbal medicine products, hydrogen washing products, and other applications.

Muqi also established a co-creation service model in 2021. Working with partners, we provide one-stop support covering solid-state hydrogen product development, design and planning, and intellectual property system development.

Our 8,000-ton annual production line and ISO 9000 quality management certification provide the manufacturing foundation for commercial delivery.

04 / Global reach

Built on knowledge, expressed through action.

Muqi consistently practices the value of “knowledge and action for the benefit of others”. We promote a culture of learning and execution among employees and customers, and we put the idea of mutual benefit into practice.

Since its founding, the company has provided “filial piety salaries” for employees’ parents and established the Muqi Fund to support employees in need. For customers, Muqi remains committed to providing comprehensive enabling services throughout the cooperation process.
