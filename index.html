World-Leading Solid-State Hydrogen
ICR Technology

ICR (Intelligent Control Dissolution Release) is MUQI's proprietary third-generation solid-state hydrogen technology. Through precise formulation of hydrogen-producing materials and natural minerals, it achieves hydrogen release & mineral dissolution, hydrogen-mineral synergy, and long-term hydrogen retention.

Zero power consumption · 18–24h hydrogen half-life · 35–50% more cost-effective than SPE/PEM electrolysis.
