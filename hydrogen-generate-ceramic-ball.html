Hydrogen Generate Ceramic Ball

ORP Ceramic Ball · Negative ORP · Antioxidant

The Muqi Hydrogen Generate Ceramic ball is a functional ceramic material composed of a variety of mineral materials and alloy materials, and is made through low-temperature baking. It adopts nanotechnology and micropore interconnection technology, solving the defects of other hydrogen production materials such as oxidation failure and the precipitation of large amounts of sediment. It can be widely used in industries such as cups and kettles, water purification, bathing, beauty care, and agricultural preservation.

Key Features

  • Produce Hydrogen in Water: Hydrogen concentration of 1200ppb
  • Reduce Oxidation Reduction Potential: Reduce water ORP from +200mv to 0mv to -200mv
  • Remove Residual Chlorine: Strong reducing ability, removes various strong oxidants
  • Remove Heavy Metal Ions: Adsorbs lead, mercury, copper, nickel, cadmium, arsenic, antimony
  • Remove Hydrogen Sulfide: Converts into water-insoluble sulfide salts
  • Remove Suspended Particles: Effectively removes particles less than 50μm
  • Add Minerals: Increases pH, adds calcium, magnesium, potassium
  • Inhibits Microbial Growth: Releases zinc ions for microorganism control
