MQ Tech — Leading Water Media Manufacturer

Ceramic ball & application solutions for health and eco-friendly living.

Muqi Technology is a leading manufacturer specializing in advanced mineral biology ceramic water media for diverse scenarios applications. The company focuses on the development of mineral hydrogen ingredients and supply chain of applications.

The company has developed a series of mineral ions slow-release biomaterials water media: Mineral hydrogen-generating ceramic ball, Multi-alloy antibacterial ceramic ball, Condensate neutralizer ceramic ball etc. Spot on health and environmentally-friendly product applications have been developed by integrating the characteristics of these new mineral materials.

Muqi Technology has established a laboratory for new materials in solid-state hydrogen storage and generate. Through collaboration with various research institutions and university, the company obtaining multiple national authorized invention patents. These innovations underscore Muqi's strong research capabilities and significant influence within the industry. Muqi has set operation offices in Jinan, Zibo and Hangzhou city respectively.
